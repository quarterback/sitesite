Ron Bronson

Choice Architect. Service Designer.

Fan of Pessoa and Finnish baseball. Can recommend a good jazz album & loose-leaf tea.

Work

Organizer of Aggregate (#GGRGT), a conference for experience designers & strategists. Founder of Indiana Design & Innovation Week.

Ron wrote Web Management for Regular People. He's led workshops & given presentations around the world at events like Design & Content (Vancouver), Pixel Up! (Cape Town), Confab Central & University of Illinois Web Conference.

Most recently, he was responsible for UX strategy, DesignOps, and content development for the City of Bloomington (IN) website redesign. Previously, he led the digital strategy team for a state college system.

He was also co-founder and head of product at Omnivore, a startup sneaker brand.
