About

These days, my practice is focused on human-centered, service & content design. At the end of 2017, I joined 18F, an office working with federal agencies to improve the UX of government.

Previously, I spent over a decade working on products & projects for a host of government, agencies & non-profits all over the U.S.

In 2017, I wrote Web Management for Regular People.You can read it for free on Gitbook.

A handful of folks know me for designing the hybrid field sport Tennis Polo (Toccer) in 2004.
