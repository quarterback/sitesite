About

Ron is a content designer at 18F. Previously, He founded the Indianpolis chapter of the Interaction Design Association, co-founded the Bloomington Code for America brigade, and launched Indiana Design Week.

Past work include leading digital teams in higher education and government. Ron enjoys working with stakeholders and demystifying digital experiences. He's been on stage at conferences on three continents, giving talks and curating workshops on topics like service design, content strategy , stakeholder engagement, digital leadership and beyond.

Ron designed Tennis Polo (Toccer), a field-racquet hybrid sport that's been played in over a dozen countries. More recently, he made Pensulo, a card deck for tackling creative blocks. He also launched Aggregate Conference, co-founded Omnivore, a startup shoe brand, and served as policy director of Future Wyoming, a think-tank devising solutions for Wyoming's brain drain from 2007-2013.

You can contact Ron via email if you're needing a skeeball lesson. He's a native of New Jersey, has lived all over the U.S., and is really into fan of Finnish Baseball.
