About

My practice is focused on human-centered, experience strategy & content design. I've spent my career exploring the ways we can remove friction from mundane experiences.

Currently, I work at 18F, a federal office collaborating to improve the UX of government. Previously, I spent over a decade leading teams and building products for a host of organizations.

I'm on the board of directors of several non-profits, played (and later coached) college tennis, and will happily talk to you about loose-leaf tea, Finnish baseball, west coast jazz, or the professional skeeball circuit. Additionally, a handful of folks know me for inventing the hybrid field sport Tennis Polo (Toccer) in 2004.

In 2017, I wrote Web Management for Regular People. You can read it for free on Gitbook. For many years, I wrote posts on my blog and on Medium.
