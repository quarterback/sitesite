Ron Bronson

Product (IxD + Service) Design Manager. Brunch enthusiast. Skeeball pro. Based in Portland.

Currently, I'm managing the product design team at 18F, a tech transformation team inside the federal government. Before that, I spent a decade building websites & managing digital teams across state & local governments around the country. I volunteer with a few groups in Portland and coach high school tennis, outside of work.


Previously, I started Indianapolis Design Week, IxDA Indianapolis, and was VP of Partnerships & board member for AIGA Indianapolis. I also served on the board of community radio station WFHB-FM and was a radio news anchor for two years. In 2004, I created a sport called Tennis Polo (Toccer).
