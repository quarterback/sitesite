Ron Bronson

Ron Bronson is an experience designer, information architect, digital director, and speaker. He works on large-scale digital transformation projects, collaborates with developers & other designers to build digital products, interfaces, and migrate large sites successfully. He's been on stage at conferences around the world discussing diverse topics around the user experience.

Before moving to the west coast, Ron started Indianapolis Design Week, and served on the board of AIGA Indy. He also spent two years as a volunteer news anchor (and later, a board member) at community radio station WFHB-FM A long time ago, he spent four years on active duty in the U.S Air Force, too.
